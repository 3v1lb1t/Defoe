Sree Mahadeva Kshethra, the traditional and mythological Kirathamoorthy temple, where the presiding deity is Kirathamoorthy bhava of Lord Shiva, who granted his deadly weapon “Pashupatha Asthra” to Arjunan for the protection of the righteousness. Situated infront of the quiet flowing Meenachil River, the temple faces east where the bright rays of the rising sun shine over the entire part which enhances the elegance and divine power of the temple lamps. The sacred place is located 1.5 km away from Punnathura, nearer to Ettumanoor-Pala highway in Kottayam district.

The ancient and mythological Kiratha temple exhibits Kirathamoorthy bhava of Lord Shiva. Devotees are eager to get a glimpse of Lord Shiva and attain his blessings and benediction. The believers are of firm opinion that the darsan at this temple will enhance the progress and prosperity to them and their kit and kin. The serenity and sacredness of this ancient and traditional temple enhances the beauty and holiness of Punnathura.
