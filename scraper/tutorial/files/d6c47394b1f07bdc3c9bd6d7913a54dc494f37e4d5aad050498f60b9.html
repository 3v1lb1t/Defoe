OUR VISION

With an objective to enhance the standard of higher education, inculcate social and moral values and scientific temper in the students, encourage the hidden talents in other fields and develop consciousness for quality education this college has made a steady progress in academics, non-academics and in infrastructure as well. Working on its mission, the institution is committed to produce the future citizens of the nation, intellectually alive and socially responsible. In all these years the college has been successful to a greater extent in fulfilling the dreams and aspirations of the founding members, donors, and education lovers. However, the journey still continues. A lot has been achieved and there is still more to do.

OUR MISSION

Remuna Degree College

Remuna is a scenic spot among the religious places of Odisha.It is basically known as Gopal Brindaban. The chief attraction of the place is the shrine of Loard Gopinath .Remuna is well known by load Gargeswar Mahadev.Sri Ram chandi,Saptasara and Devi Astadasabhuja give recognition of the religious consciousness of Remuna to the whole world .It is believed that Lord Ramchandra brought out a water stream by casting arrow for the bath of Devi Sita. The history of Remuna is originated from the mythology of ancient India. Remuna Degree College was setup with a hope for spreading education, mostly for the girl students in and around the locality. The seed was planted by Late Jayanta Ghosh a renowned personality and a reputed journalist not only of Balasore but also of Odisha. Some other educationist and social workers of this constituency came forward for the development of this institution .The college was affiliated to Fakir Mohan University with 256 seats in Arts,48 seats in Commerce and 128 seats in Science. Science is comprises of PCM & CBZ worker of this constituency. came forward for the development of this institution of 80 and 48 respectively. Remuna Degree College was set up in Remuna in1996 with a view to facilitate higher Education to the students of the area as its vicinity. It is a needless to mention that due to the involvement of the staff of this college,the college has unique achievement over the years in academic and other allied spheres. Remuna Degree College is also affiliated under UGC scoring 2f and 12B .UGC has also spread its hand for funding into several developmental works.

THE CREST

The crest of our college comprise of :-          

                   1. At the top inside- Name of the college
                   2. Inside right- bat, ball and stump a burning torch and
                   3. Inside left- an experimental flask
                   4. At the middle- -an opened book with the burning lamp
                   5. At the bottom inside – a floating boat
                   6. At the bottom outside the crest :-The motto

MOTTO:-

The book and the burning lamp symbolises the dissemination of learning and wisdom which is the prime motto of the institution. The experiment flask on the stand symbolizes the promotion of science among the learner . The bat ball and stump symbolizes the inspiration among students for sports and games.

The floating boat on the waves of the ocean signifies.          

1. The marine glories of the region
2. The excellence of our ancestral trade and commerce
3. The never ending aspirations of our students
4. The motto at the bottom of the emblem:Knowledge,discipline,dedication,indicates higher education ,imparting discipline among students and to eradicate the darkness of ignorance andilliteracy among students .
