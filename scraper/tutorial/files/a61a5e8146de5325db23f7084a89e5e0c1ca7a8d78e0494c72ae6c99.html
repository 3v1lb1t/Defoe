Welcome!

Welcome to the new web portal of the Gozo Culture and Information Office within the Minstry for Gozo.

The aim of this portal is to promote and bring to you cultural events, information and all that is happening in the Maltese Islands focusing mainly on those related and occurring on the Island of Gozo.

While we're still adding and fine tuning material to add to this new website, we would love to hear what you have to say, wish to see, be informed about and even what in your opinion should be added to this portal. To do so, send us your comment / suggestion using the form below. Rest assured we value what you have to say...

So stay tuned and check out our website regularly for newly added and updated information.

You can also interact with us through our Facebook pages GozoCulture.com and Lejlet Lapsi - Notte Gozitana, be our friend on Gozo Culture or follow us on Gozo Culture.
