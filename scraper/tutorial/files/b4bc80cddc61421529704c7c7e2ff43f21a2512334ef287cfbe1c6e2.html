Welcome to Our Website

The holy cross Academy, Karma Road, Aurangabad (Bihar) is situated in the heart of the town to cater the need of education of the children of Aurangabad district. It is an institution to fulfill the desires and aspirations of the people. In the past the students of Aurangabad earned name and fame in education. This institution also wants to achieve the previous glory with a view to bring balance and harmonious development of the citizens and high sense of partriotism, this institution is found.
We believe in the dignity and worth of students and recognize the importance of their responsibilities to their fellow men in the world communities.
We believe that change is a consistent factor in life. Therefore, education should encourage in the student the development of personal valus and thinking process which facilitate their intelligent adaptability to change the society.
It is our the best endeavour to develop in each student the following:-
1. A mastery of the skill of communication and learning.
2. The ability to think cleary, logically and independently.
3. Good health and physical fitness enabling the student to live an energetic wholesome and productive life.
4.Appreciation of ethical and cultural value.
