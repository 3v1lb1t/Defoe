My High Life


I wanted to die 
but...Death said "life has something more stored for you"
I pleaded with life..
To say what it was..
Life said "half of the play is still left"
I asked to myself..
If death didn't want me then..
And if half of the play was still left..
And if something was stored for me..
Alas!!Where's the happiness..
That I was promised for?
What went wrong-- the expectation? the play?
Or someone broke into my store room..?
-Yodin Limbu
