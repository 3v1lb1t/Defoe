Floridian Partners, LLC, is a full-service, Florida-based consulting firm operating throughout the state of Florida. The firm’s core services include government relations, public affairs and crisis management. We operate offices in Tallahassee, Fort Lauderdale and Miami and provide services at all levels of government. We have the relationships and know-how to access key decision makers on any given client matter, whether to impact proposed state legislation, a local or state agency regulation, local government ordinance, the state budget, or a local or state purchasing opportunity.

The strength of our firm lies in the sophisticated professionals who form our team and bring together a wider range of subject matter expertise, as well as strong government advocacy, communications and crisis management experience. We recognize that our clients are not just looking for “lobbying,” but need a firm that understands politics, policy, public-private partnership, stakeholder engagement, public opinion, corporate reputation and business strategy.

Our client base is varied and ranges from Fortune 500 companies to non-profit agencies, local governments and sole proprietorships. We understand that our success is measured by the success of our clients, and our mission is to provide the highest level of professional service and deliver results.
