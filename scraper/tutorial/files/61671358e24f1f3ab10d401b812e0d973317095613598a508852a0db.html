Overview


Leadership Opportunities

We believe in raising leaders regardless of age, as long as you possess the right passion, attitude and drive.  Our group of young and dynamic individuals is on accelerated track to be leaders of Today and Tomorrow in their respective field of works.

Inspiring & Challenging Work Environment

We seek to bring out the best in people through cultivating an inspiring and challenging work environment.  We take pride in achieving great things in the fast-paced, ever-changing internet and entertainment business.


  

Positive Organizational Culture

A positive organizational culture is one that is oriented towards success, encourages creativity and places emphasis on teamwork. We aim to build this by placing a high priority on healthy relationships among people.

People Are Our Greatest Asset

We value people. We believe in attracting the crème de la crème because the greatest asset in an organization is its people.

 

Work Hard. Play Harder.

All work and no play will make Garena a boring gaming company! In Garena, we work really hard at what we do but we play even harder.  Simply put, we want to be really good at things we do, be it at work, or in games.  

Professional Development

We take interest in the professional development of our staff so that they can grow together with the company. This is done through our extensive talent development and training programs.
