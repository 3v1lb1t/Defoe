Peru Holidays

On October 4, 2010, in Media, by admin

The trip was great and yes the changes were inevitable. On hindsight, I think the agents in Peru acted very efficiently when they booked us on the flight. Please pass our sincere thanks to them and all the people in Peru who looked after us – they all did a great job.

I think that I have fallen in love with the country and would be happy to return there anytime. The people are warm and friendly and the scenery is out of this world. The only comment I would make is that please warn people about the really early morning starts and that the long bus journey from Nasca to Arequipa has no stops.

May I also thank you sincerely for responding to my e-mails and phone calls so promptly and for taking care of everything.
